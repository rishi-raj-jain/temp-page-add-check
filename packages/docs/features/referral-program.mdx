Creem's Referral Program lets you earn rewards by inviting other merchants to join the platform. When a business you refer signs up and reaches their qualification threshold, you receive a one-time reward credit straight to your Creem balance.

## How It Works

The referral program is straightforward:

1. **Share your unique link** - Get your personalized referral link from the Referrals dashboard and share it with potential merchants
2. **They sign up through your link** - When a merchant clicks your link and creates a Creem store, they're attributed to you
3. **They qualify** - Once they've processed at least $1,000 in settled payments and completed verification, they qualify
4. **You get paid** - You receive your $100 reward within 30 days of qualification, credited to your Creem balance

## Getting Your Referral Link

Your referral link is available in your Creem dashboard under **Balances → Referrals**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Dashboard">
    Sign in to your Creem dashboard and navigate to **Balances** in the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Referrals">
    Click the **Referrals** tab. If this is your first time, you'll see the program overview and your link will be generated automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Your Link or Code">
    You can share either:
    - **Referral Link**: A full URL (e.g., `https://creem.io/join/A1B2C3D4`) — easiest for emails and websites
    - **Referral Code**: Just the code (e.g., `A1B2C3D4`) — convenient for social media and conversations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share It">
    Use the built-in buttons to copy your link, or share directly to X or LinkedIn.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Program Details

### Rewards and Qualification

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Reward per referral** | $50 USD (or equivalent in other currencies) |
| **Qualifying volume** | $1,000 in settled payments |
| **Payout timing** | Within 30 days of qualification |
| **Hold period** | 30 days (covers chargebacks and refunds) |

### What Counts as a Referral

A referral qualifies when:

- ✅ The merchant is **new to Creem** and signs up through your link
- ✅ They **create a store** and complete KYC verification
- ✅ They **process at least $1,000** in settled payments
- ❌ Self-referrals don't count (referring yourself from another account)
- ❌ Duplicate accounts don't count (referring the same person twice)

### Referral Statuses

Your referrals progress through these statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Merchant signed up through your link and created a store, but hasn't yet qualified |
| **Qualified** | Merchant has processed the required $1,000 in settled payments |
| **Rewarded** | Reward has been credited to your balance and is available to withdraw |
| **Rejected** | Referral was rejected due to fraud, policy violations, or other issues — no reward paid |
| **Reversed** | Reward was previously credited but reversed due to chargebacks, refunds, or account closure |

## Understanding Holds and Reversals

To protect both you and the platform, rewards are subject to a 30-day hold period after qualification. Here's why:

### Risk Holds

Merchants can issue refunds or chargebacks up to 180 days after a transaction. The 30-day hold ensures we have time to identify problems before crediting your reward. Holds cover:

- **Refunds**: If a customer of the referred merchant requests a refund
- **Chargebacks**: If a cardholder disputes a transaction
- **Cancellations**: If the referred merchant closes their account

### Reversals

If a reward has already been credited to your balance, it can still be reversed if:

- The referred merchant issues chargebacks that exceed the hold period
- The referred merchant's account is closed or suspended
- Fraud or policy violations are discovered

Reversed rewards are deducted from your available balance. If your balance goes negative, future payments are held until the balance recovers.

## Tracking Your Referrals

The **Referrals** dashboard shows:

### Stats at a Glance

- **Total Earned**: Sum of all rewarded referrals (in USD or your local currency)
- **Pending Rewards**: Number of referrals currently on hold awaiting credit
- **Merchants Referred**: Total count of all referrals (pending, qualified, and rewarded)

### Referral Activity Table

A detailed table of every referral with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Merchant** | Referred store name and owner email |
| **Status** | Current referral status (pending, qualified, rewarded, etc.) |
| **Reward** | Amount earned (or "—" if not yet qualified) |
| **Date** | When the referral was created |

You can sort and paginate through the table to track all your referrals.

## Payout Mechanics

### When You Get Paid

Referral rewards are automatically added to your **Available Balance** on the Balances page, where they appear alongside your payment processing earnings.

### Normal Payout Schedule

Referral rewards follow the same payout schedule as all your Creem balance:

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Payout windows** | 1st and 15th of each month |
| **Minimum balance** | 50 USD or 50 EUR |
| **Processing time** | 7–12 days after payout date |

To request a payout:
1. Go to **Balances** → **Withdraw**
2. Ensure your balance is above the minimum threshold
3. Click **Withdraw** — your request is queued for the next payout window

### Multiple Currencies

If your referrals are in a different currency than your payout method:
- Creem will convert at the current exchange rate
- Your payout provider (bank or crypto service) may apply an additional conversion fee
- This is outside Creem's control

## Compliance and Restrictions

### Who Can Use the Referral Program

✅ **Can participate:**
- Active Creem stores in good standing
- Individuals who've completed identity verification (KYC)
- Any merchant, regardless of location (subject to local laws)

❌ **Cannot participate:**
- Accounts under investigation for fraud or policy violations
- Merchants who've been suspended or banned
- Self-referrals and accounts created to game the program

### Program Rules

To keep the program fair and secure:

- **No paid advertising** - You can't buy Google Ads or social media ads specifically for your referral link
- **No misleading claims** - Don't promise outcomes or rewards beyond what Creem offers
- **No spam** - Don't send unsolicited bulk emails or messages
- **No account abuse** - Creating fake accounts to earn rewards will disqualify all referrals

Violations of these rules may result in:
- Rejection of referrals
- Reversal of previously earned rewards
- Suspension from the referral program
- Account suspension (in severe cases)

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get my referral link?">
    Sign in to your Creem dashboard, go to **Balances → Referrals**, and your link will be generated automatically on the first visit. You can copy the full link or just the code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use multiple referral links?">
    No, each store gets one referral link. If you have multiple stores, each gets its own link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I refer someone who already has a Creem account?">
    No, the referral must be a new merchant signing up for the first time through your link. Existing customers don't count.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long is the hold period?">
    All referrals have a 30-day hold after qualification. This covers potential refunds and chargebacks during this window. After 30 days, the reward is credited and appears on your Available Balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a merchant refunds their customers?">
    Refunds from the referred merchant's customers don't disqualify the referral (they already qualified). However, if refunds are large enough or chargebacks occur, the referral may be reversed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my referred merchant closes their account?">
    If a merchant closes their account, any reward already credited will be reversed. If they close before qualifying, the referral stays pending indefinitely.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I get paid for my own referrals?">
    No, self-referrals don't count. You can't refer your own second store or account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how much I can earn?">
    No limit. You can refer as many merchants as you want and earn unlimited rewards. Creem celebrates your success!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When do I see the referral in my dashboard?">
    The referral appears in your dashboard immediately after the merchant signs up through your link. It starts in **Pending** status.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see which of my referrals generated revenue?">
    Yes, the referral activity table shows each merchant's status. Once they process $1,000 and qualify, the status changes to **Qualified** and the reward is shown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a referral is rejected?">
    Rejected referrals mean the merchant didn't meet program requirements or violated policies. No reward is paid. Check the referral details or contact support if you have questions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I contact support about my referrals?">
    Reach out to Creem support via the help center or contact page. Provide your store ID and the referral code if you're asking about a specific referral.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
